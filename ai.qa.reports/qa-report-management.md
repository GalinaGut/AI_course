Regression results indicate high overall stability with a 92% pass rate; however, there is moderate release risk due to 2 critical defects and concentrated failures in the Checkout and Orders modules, which affect key user flows. While Profile and Login are fully stable, existing flaky tests reduce confidence in test reliability. It is recommended to resolve critical issues, validate fixes through targeted regression testing of impacted modules, and address flaky tests before proceeding with release.
